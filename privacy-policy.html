What data do we collect?

The app collects the following data:

  • All data contained in the EU COVID Digital Certificates(Name, Date of Birth, ...)
  • Timestamp when you accepted The "Terms and conditions" and the "Privacy Policy".

How does the app collect your data?

You directly provide the app with the data contained in the EU COVID Digital Certificates. The app collects those data when you:

  • Import a File (Permission for files and media is required)
  • Scan a QR-Code (Permission for the camera is required)
  • Share data with the app

the app saves the timestamp every time you accpt the "Terms and conditions" and the "Privacy Policy".

How your data will be used?

The app collects your data so that it is able to:

  • Show you the data on the display of the device
  • Inform you on updates of the "Terms and conditions" and the "Privacy Policy"

How do we store your data?

The app stores your data locally on the device in an encrypted format. The data is only stored locally in an encrypted format and does not leave the device unless someone scans the QR code displayed in the app.

The app will keep your data as long as the app is installed on the device.

Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request for copies of your personal data.

All personal data collected by the app is stored locally only.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that any information you believe is inaccurate gets corrected. You also have the right to request to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The app displays only the data you entered, therefore you can delete them and insert new ones.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that your personal data gets erased, under certain conditions.

You are able to erase all your data directly in the app.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that the processing of your personal data get restricted, under certain conditions.

All your data is stored and processed locally on your device and you can delete the app with its data.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

All your data is stored and processed locally on your device and you can delete the app with its data.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that the data that the app has collected gets transfered to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

All the data that the app has collected from you is displayed directly in the app and can thus be shared by you with others.
